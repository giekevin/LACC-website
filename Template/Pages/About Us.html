Our Vision Statement "Growing and Reaching Out Together"

Our Mission Statement "To Know Christ and To Make Him Known"

  • To Share Jesus Christ In Order To Bring People To Christ
  • To Be Family Oriented

A clear God-given vision is an essential aspect of our becoming the church that God intends.

We keep our bearings in Christ by:

  • Maintaining a clear sense of who we are (i.e. our congregational form of government), our heritage, and our God-given strengths.
  • Practicing effective communication in order to strengthen our identity and increase the unity, fellowship and cooperation within our fellowship.
  • Cultivating passionate leadership which will build confidence and support. This is achieved by developing the strengths and skills of members and adherents for ministry inside and outside the congregation.
  • Upholding accountability to clear biblical standards for leaders and members for the glory of God.
  • Understanding that freedom and liberty go hand in hand with responsibility for the good of the whole.
  • Cherishing an inclusive fellowship which embraces differences in a biblical manner.
