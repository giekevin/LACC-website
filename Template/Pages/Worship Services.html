Our Sunday morning worship experience includes:

  • singing praise hymns, both old and new
  • time for corporate and personal prayer
  • and inspiring sermons

Children leave for Childrens' Church part way through the service.

The worship service is followed by coffee and fellowship in the lower hall.

We have a monthly Communion Service on the first Sunday of the month, which is open to all who profess Jesus Christ as Lord and Saviour.
